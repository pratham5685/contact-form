Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a vulnerability audit take?

The audit typically takes 3-5 business days, depending on the complexity and size of your website. We provide detailed reports and actionable insights after the audit is complete.

How are your services different from automated tools?

Unlike automated tools, we provide personalized, manual audits carried out by our experienced cybersecurity experts. Automated tools may miss critical vulnerabilities, while our team offers a thorough, hands-on approach to identifying complex issues and providing actionable solutions.

Is my data secure during the audit?

Yes, your data is fully secure during the audit. We follow industry-standard security protocols and comply with all privacy regulations to ensure that your information remains confidential. Our team handles your data with the utmost care and discretion.

What if I don’t want to proceed after the free audit?

If you choose not to proceed after the free audit, there is no obligation. You are free to walk away with the audit report and the actionable insights provided. We believe in transparent services, so there is no pressure to continue with our paid services.
